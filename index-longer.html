I have worked on productions with the BBC, Salford City Football Club and the British Film Institution amongst other organisations.

I graduated from the University of Salford with a 1st in Professional Sound and Video Technology. And went on to study MA Wildlife Documentary Production.

I am well versed in many aspects of media, but specialise in camera operation and video editing. I have experience with a wide range of cameras from GoPros and DSLRs to Canon C200 and Sony FS7s. I am proficient in the use of a variety of nonlinear editing software including: Adobe Premiere Pro, Davinci Resolve and Avid Media Composer, as well as digital audio workstations such as Avid ProTools, Apple Logic Pro X and Steinberg Cubase.
